## How It Works

Each day, 3 teams cover the main zones (1 team per zone) and 1 team floats.
The float team prioritizes BLD and unofficial events, and only helps main zones
when the main team can't fill all slots.

With 14 judges per group:
- Main team alone covers most groups (~19 workers available per group)
- Float team provides 1-5 extra workers per group when needed
- In edge cases (parallel events consuming float), judges drop to 10-13 (min flex)
